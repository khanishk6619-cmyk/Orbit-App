Features
Built for real life
Everything the top wellness apps have — in one free, no-download experience.
🪐

Life Score

A single 0–100 score calculated daily from your mood, sleep, stress, habits, water and tasks. Know exactly how you're doing at a glance.

🤖

AI Assistant

Chat with Orbit AI to log meals, add tasks, set mood, get health advice, or just talk. It reads your live data and responds with context.

🍽️

Nutrition Tracking

Log meals and get instant calorie + macro estimates (protein, carbs, fat). Daily goal progress bar. Full meal history.

🔥

Habit Streaks

Build daily habits with streak tracking, a 30-day grid, and push notifications that remind you before your streak breaks.

😴

Sleep Tracking

Log sleep quality and duration. Wearable device sync support (Apple Watch, Whoop, Fitbit, Garmin). 7-day sleep chart.

💪

Workout Logger

8 workout types with calorie burn estimates. Weekly activity chart, best streak, and full workout history. Device sync supported.

🎯

Smart Tasks

Priority matrix (High / Medium / Low), due dates with overdue alerts, and a Pomodoro focus timer. Productivity, sorted.

🧘

Stress & Breathing

Log stress level 1–10. Four guided breathing modes: 4-7-8, Box, 2-2, Deep. 7-day stress trend chart. SOS calm-down button.

🔔

Push Notifications

Real browser notifications for habit reminders, water check-ins, workout nudges, evening summaries, and streak protection.

⚖️

Weight & BMI

Log weight daily, visualise 90-day trends, and get your BMI calculated automatically from your height and latest entry.

🔴🟢🔵

Activity Rings

Apple Watch-style rings on your home screen. Move (calories), Hydrate (water), Rest (sleep) — all in one glance.

📓

Journal + Gratitude

Daily journal entries with AI insights, gratitude prompts, and a full entry history. Writing is the highest-ROI habit.
